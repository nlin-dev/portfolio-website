My passion for optimization problems is what first led me to have an interest in statistics and machine learning and it still serves as the foundation that influences both my work and interests to this day. Because of this, my primary focus is in developing AI solutions that tackle real-world challenges.

Outside of work, I am a national-level powerlifting coach and powerlifter, which I devote a significant portion of my time to. In my opinion athletic performance is a complex optimization problem that I approach individually with each of my athletes. If you want to learn more about my mathematical approach to athletic performance, please check out my powerlifting page!

I spend the majority of my remaining time researching predictive markets, in particular financial markets, as the underlying technical elements in these fields are built upon rigorous statistical theory. Because of this, most of the projects I work on in my free time involve building and optimizing various financial predictive models.
