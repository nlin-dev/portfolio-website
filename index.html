My Personal Background

I am a senior at New York University studying Computer Science and Data Science. I am passionate about machine and deep learning and in implementing these algorithms to address societal inequalities.

I will be attending Columbia University in the Fall to pursue a Master of Science in Learning Analytics. Given how impactful big data can be, my intent is to explore how harnessing these capabilities could address fundamental inequalities such as unequal access to education. Because of this, I am incredibly excited to gain experience and exposure in practically applying data science solutions in real world contexts.
